Random Events

Who Doesn’t like surprises? That’s what random events are all about—strange events, characters, or creatures that come out of nowhere. Sometimes a random event can lead to a reward, and sometimes it can lead to trouble! Oh, well. Life would be boring if there were no surprises at all, right? Here’s a look at the kinds of random events you can expect in the game. There are four types of random events; Guardians, Gift Givers and Helpers, Danger! and Just Plain Annoying. On the bottom and the left of the screen you will notice a list of random events, click on the one you wish to learn about and I will redirect you.

Guardians

As you travel around the Runescape world, you will come across some places that are protected. These Guardians are usually pretty powerful, but some of them have weaknesses, or if you have level 43 prayer you can protect yourself from any of their attacks which gives you an unfair advantage over them. But if you don't have a high enough prayer level and combat level, your best bet is to run away from them. They wont chase you for very long. Of course if you beat them, they might drop some interesting items for you to collect.
