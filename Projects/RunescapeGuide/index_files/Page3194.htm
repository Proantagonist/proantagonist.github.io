About Karamja Island

After you buy your fishing equipment at Port Sarim, pay 30 GP for a boat ride to Karamja Island for some serious high-level fishing. You can cage fishing here for lobsters, and harpoon tuna and swordfish. But this exotic island is about more than fish. Take a walk through the jungle and check out the snakes and monkeys. You can find lot of imps here, too. Maybe they like frenetic energy on the island—random events seem to happen a lot on the fishing dock! If you want to escape the island for calmer ground, make sure you have another 30 GP on you. The fare to get to the island covers only one-way trip! It’s worth noting that most of Karamja is accessible to members only, but there are many options that will make the trip worthwhile to free players as well.
