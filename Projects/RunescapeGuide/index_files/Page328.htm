About This Strategy Guide

If you are just starting Runescape, the huge world can be overwhelming.

This Guide will help you make sense of all the different aspects of Runescape, including:

 

Skills: We’ll tell you what the basic skill in Runescape are and how to master them

 

 

Combat: Runescape Players love to battle. We’ll tell you what to do  if you’re attacked– and how to master the skills of a warrior.

 

 

Quest: We won’t tell you how to complete your quest– that would spoil the fun, and besides its too much typing to do. But we will give you some tips and hints to help you on your way.

 

 

Characters and Creatures: Get the scoop about the many people and monsters you’ll encounter in Runescape.

 

 

Maps: Getting around Runescape can be tricky if you don't know the land. But these maps will give you somewhat some help.

 

 

Glossary: Until you get used to the Runescape lingo, check this quick guide to find out what everybody is talking about.
