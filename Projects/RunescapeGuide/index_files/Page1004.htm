Why Do I Need Armor?

If you’re going to be in a battle, you’re going to need some armor to protect you. You can buy armor in a shop like the Grand Exchange, learn how to make it with the skills of crafting and smithing, or simply buy from another players, although it is always going to be a cheaper or more expensive. The kind of armor you wear depends on the kind of fighting you want to do.
