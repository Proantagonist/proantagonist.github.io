About Draynor Village

When you are just starting out, Draynor Village is the place to go fish for food. Here, fishermen with the lowest XP can cast their nets and build up fishing XP. You won’t find too many places to shop in this town , but you will find many people who can do things for you.

Places to shop

Diango Toy Store, although its more a cart than a real shop.

Diango can return holiday items if you’ve lost them.

Places of Interest

Aggie the Witch, a dye maker

Ned, a sailor and master rope maker-will gladly sell you some.
