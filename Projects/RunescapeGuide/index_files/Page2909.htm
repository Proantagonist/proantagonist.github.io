Zombie

Height: 5 ft. 6in.

Weight: 180 lbs.

Varieties: Various levels.

Locations: Varrock sewers and other hidden locations.

Description: These terrifying creatures crave flesh and will attack any living thing that dares to come near them.

When you see one you should:Hold your nose! Nothing smells worse than a living corpse. Oh, and you should probably run too—fast!
