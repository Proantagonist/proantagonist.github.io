Guide to Runescape Quests

Witch’s Potion

Difficulty Level: Easy

Requirements: None

Location of Quest:

In Hetty’s house in Rimmington.

Who Are You Doing the Quest For?

Hetty the witch.

How Do You Begin This Quest?

Visit Hetty the witch and ask her for a quest. When she questions you, answer yes.

Object of Quest: To get some potion ingredients for Hetty: eye of newt, a rat’s tail, an onion, and a burn piece of meat.

 

 

 

Reward: 1 QP and 365 magic XP.

Tip: You wont have to go far for Rimmington to get the ingredients you need.
