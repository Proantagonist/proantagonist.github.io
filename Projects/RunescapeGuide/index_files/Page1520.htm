Guide to Runescape Quests

Demon Slayer

Difficulty Level: Difficult

Requirements: You should be able to defeat an apocalyptic demon.

Location of Quest:

Varrock Square, next to the clothing shop and west of the Varrock fountain.

Who Are You Doing the Quest For?

The Gypsy.

How Do You Begin This Quest?

When the Gypsy asks if she can read your future, say yes!

Object of Quest: A mighty demon has been summoned to destroy the city of Varrock. The Gypsy tells you that you are the one destined to stop him!

 

 

 

Reward: 3 QP and the silverlight sword.

Tip: Killing Chickens is a quick way to get bones you will need for this quest.
