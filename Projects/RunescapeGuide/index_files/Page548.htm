Using Your Controls

The Runescape tutorial will teach you the basic skills you need to know to get around the game. But There are a few additional features you will use again and again. Here are some tips to get you familiar with these controls.

 

Camera Controls

You can rotate the camera on your screen to get the best view of the action. Sometimes its necessary to see objects that you'd otherwise miss. Rotating the camera is easy.

 

1. Press the LEFT and RIGHT arrows to rotate the camera from left to right.

2. Use the UP and DOWN arrows keys to adjust the vertical height of the camera. With this feature, you can move from a behind-the-player view to a bird’s-eye view from above.

 

Trading with Other Players

Why should you trade items with other players? You can usually sell or buy your items at a better price than the shops offer. You can also get items through trading that might not be available in the shops, or you can visit Varrock where the Grand Exchange is located. If you don't know where Varrock is look in the “Maps” section of this guide. Once in the Grand Exchange you will be give a tutorial with a guide. Follow these easy steps to learn to trade with other players.

 

1. Find a buyer or a seller. If you have mined coal and want to sell it, go to a busy area or world and yell, “Coal for Sale!” or something that will potentially attract a buyer.

 

2. If somebody wants to buy coal, right-click on their character. Select the “Trade” option. The other player will be informed of your request. They will click on a purple-pink colored message in their chat window, this will also appear in yours if another player wishes to trade you.

 

3. A screen will appear. The items on the left are what you are offering to trade and the ones on the right are the ones the other player is offering to trade. To add an item to your offer, click on an item on your inventory box, which will be displayed automatically along with the trade screen. To add multiples of the same objects, such as money, runes arrows, noted items, etc. Right click on the item you wish to offer in multiples, and select the option “Offer X” and type the number you wish to offer and press enter. If you want to remove items from the trade screen, simply click on the item in the trade screen and it will e removed.

 

4. Are you happy with the trade? If so press the green ACCEPT button. If not press the red DECLINE button, and the trade screen will close.

 

5. If you do accept, a final confirmation screen will a appear. This gives you one last chance to check the trade you are about to complete. Make sure you are satisfied with what is being offered. Then press ACCEPT for the last time.

 

Be Careful when Trading!

There is no way to reverse the trade once it is completed. Some players may trick you by saying they’ll give you information in return for items, or by promising to give them right back to you. It’s best not to trust offers like these.
