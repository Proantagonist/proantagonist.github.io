Mastering the Basic Skill of Woodcutting

Why Should I Master Woodcutting?

Woodcutting is just what it sounds like— cutting down trees to make logs. It’s a basic skill, but it’s essential. You need  logs to make a fire, and you need fire to cook food. You could go through the entire game cutting down ordinary trees, but it doesn't hurt to build your woodcutting XP. Let’s say you're stuck in the middle of a bunch of willow trees and you don't have enough XP to chop it down. You'd have to go searching for a lower-level tree to chop. There's another bonus, too. The higher your woodcutting XP, the more logs you’ll be able to chop. Besides woodcutting is a good way to get quick money.

How Do I Do It?

It’s pretty simple: just left-click on a tree and its that simple! Of course you need an axe in either your hand or inventory to do the cutting. The More powerful your axe, the better chance you'll have of chopping down the tree.

 

How Can I Get Axes?

You will get a free axe when you do the tutorial in the game. Axes can break fairly easily though so you'll probably need a new one before long. If you don't have an axe, you can buy one in Bob’s Brilliant Axes west of Lumbridge. You'll definitely need to upgrade your axe once your skills improve; powerful axes will allow you to chop down trees much faster.
