Why Should I Master Prayer?

Magic is one way to give you an edge in Runescape. Prayer is another. It’s a special Skill made up of entirely for the Runescape game. It can give you a boost in battle, make you stronger, restore hitpoints, and give you protection from magic, ranged and melee attacks. It doesn’t cost you anything, unless your buying your bones, and the effects don't last long.

 

How Do I Do It?

It’s easy to use prayer— just select the prayer symbol from your interface. If you are at a high enough level to use a prayer, it will be lit up. Left-click on the prayer to activate it. Your prayer points will drain when you use prayers, so head to a church to recharge your prayer points. One way to increase your prayer XP is to bury the bones of monsters you kill! (Or you can bury bones left on the ground by another player.) You can also buy bones from other players, but keep in mind that this method can be very expensive.

Can I Join a Guild

No, but if you have a prayer level of 31 or more, you can enter the monastery located west of Edgeville. You can get a prayer boost here and grab some monk’s robes, too.
