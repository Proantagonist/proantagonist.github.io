About Crandor

This tiny island is more than it looks, it contains valuable treasure for those who seek it. To gain access to this island one must first complete the Dragon Slayer Quest, after you’ve completed the quest you will have direct access from the inside of the Karamja Volcano, but be wary this land home many creatures such as Lesser Demons, Hobgoblins, Scorpions, Skeletons, and Moss Giants.
