Mastering the Basic Skill of Crafting

Why Should I Master Crafting?

Yan make a lot of GPs forging metals into weapons, and you can also use the skill of crafting to turn metals into jewelry. Players love to adorn their characters with necklaces and amulets, so a master jewelry maker can become a very wealthy player

 

Crafting also has many practical applications. If you love cooking, you can craft your own pie dishes. If you like to battle, knowing how to make leather armor can come in handy.

How Do I Do It?

Pottery: start with clay, which you can get by mining. Soften the clay with water. Take the clay to a potters wheel ( try Barbarian Village). Use the clay on the potters wheel and select what object you want to make. If you have the necessary level, you will be able to make the item. Take the item to a pottery oven to fire it. If it doesn't crack upon heating, you’ll end up with a nice piece of pottery.

 

Leather: Start with cowhides, which you can get by going to a cow field and killing some cows, or if you have money buy them in, preferably, Al-Kharid, for 100-110 gp each. Take the cowhides Elis the tanner in Al-Kharid. There you can turn the hide into normal (Soft) or hard leather. Hard leather is stronger but it cost a little more to make. Once you turn your hide into leather, you can craft it into a useful item, like a pair of boots or piece of armor. If you have enough thread in your inventory and a needle, click on the item you want to make to craft it. This is done by left-clicking the needle and then left clicking the leather, and a crafting screen will appear, there you choose what to make. 

 

Gems: If you are lucky enough to find a gem (during a Random Event, for instance) you can make it into more valuable by cutting it. You can use the cut gem when you are making gold jewelry. First, buy a chisel from a general store or crafting shop. Select the chisel from your inventory and the gem if you wish to cut it. If your crafting level in not high enough to cut a specific gem, you might want to make some pottery or leather armor to increase your XP.

 

Symbols: You can use silver bars to make symbols that will make your prayers last longer. These are called holy symbols. Use your silver bar on the furnace to make your holy symbol, but you also need a specific mould for it, these can be obtained in a crafting shop. Once the symbol is made, you will need to make some string. Use some shears (Can be bought in a general store) on a sheep to get wool. Spin the wool in the spinning wheel to make a ball. Use the ball of wool on your holy symbol to give it a string. If you have a prayer level of 31 or higher, you can take the symbol to Brother Jered in the Monastery to be blessed.

 

 

What Items Can I Craft?

Jewelry and Amulets: You need gold bars to make jewelry. The best way to get them is by mining and smithing. Once you have your bars, you can make amulets, necklaces, and rings. First, buy the mould you want from a crafting shop. Take the gold bar and mould and use the gold bar in the furnace. Then choose the item you would like to make. If you're lucky enough to have a gem, you can add this jewel to your piece if you have high enough smithing level. If you have a high enough magic level you can also enchant amulets that will give bonuses to the owner. First you need to give your amulet of string ( See Symbols above). Then use the magic skill to enchant it ( See Magic Skill).

 

Can I Join a Guild?

You can enter the crafting guild if your crafting level is at least 40. You also need to be wearing a brown apron, which can be obtained for a low price in the clothing shop in Varrock Square. The Crafting Guild is located west of Port Sarim and north of Rimmington. In the guild you will find many things to help you with your crafting including pottery over, potter’s wheels, spinning wheels, a tanner, moulds for free, gold rocks, silver rocks, and clay.
